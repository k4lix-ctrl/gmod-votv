# GM-VoTV

**Garry's Mod → Voices of the Void**  
A small-scale, self-contained cooperative fan project.

> *Not affiliated with Egor 'Korovex' Rezenov or the original Voices of the Void.  
> This is a non-commercial, fan-made experiment for a closed group of friends.*

---

## 📡 About the Project

This is not a remaster or a full conversion.  
It's an attempt to adapt the **atmosphere, signals, and base maintenance** from *Voices of the Void* into a **cooperative Garry's Mod** experience.

Played by 2–4 people. One player acts as **ASO Personnel** (events, drone control, fine-tuning). The others are **ASO Trainees** – people who perform tasks, fix generators, collect hash codes, and panic.

We don't copy the original mechanics 1:1.  
We build something that *feels right* for a late-night session with friends and a cold cup of tea.

---

## 🧠 Why does this exist?

Because *Voices of the Void* is brilliant,  
but we wanted to share the strange things with friends.

No leaderboard.
No rush.
No monetization.
Just a server where things beep at night and someone is definitely hiding in the radar room.
